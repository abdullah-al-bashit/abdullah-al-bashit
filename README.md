## Abdullah Al Bashit, PhD

**Postdoctoral Research Associate** · Bioengineering · Northeastern University

I develop signal processing and machine learning methods for biomedical applications.

### Current Research

**Alzheimer's Disease Pathology Mapping**  
Synchrotron X-ray microdiffraction for spatial mapping of fibrillar polymorphs in human brain tissue. Collaboration with Massachusetts General Hospital and Brookhaven National Laboratory.

**COPD Multi-Omic Risk Stratification**  
Integrating imaging, transcriptomics, and proteomics data from the COPDGene cohort. NIH-funded collaboration with Brigham and Women's Hospital.
